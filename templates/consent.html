Consent Form for Participation in Study

If you agree to participate in this study, you will be asked to complete the following questionnaire type tasks.

Background Questionnaire:

  • Provide basic demographic and background information.

Task 1: AI Analysis Evaluation

In this task, you will be presented with 150 images of daily candlestick charts that have been analyzed using AI. For each image, you are required to address the following questions:

  • Predict the midpoint of the next candlestick.
  • Indicate how confident you are in your prediction.
  • State whether you believe the analysis presented in the image supports your prediction.
  • Rate the clarity of the visualization.

Task 2: Candlestick Chart Analysis

In this task, you will be presented with 50 images of daily candlestick charts. For each image, you are required to:

  • Predict the midpoint of the next candlestick.
  • Indicate how confident you are in your prediction.

Estimated Time Commitment

The study will take approximately 45 minutes to complete. You will answer each section independently without external help.

Voluntary Participation

Participation is voluntary. There are no direct benefits to you, but your responses will help advance research on AI and ethical data visualization. You may stop at any time without penalty. Should you stop without completing all tasks, the data can be used to analyze the parts you have completed.

Confidentiality and Data Handling

Your responses will be kept confidential, with only basic demographic information collected. All data will be securely stored and analyzed anonymously.
